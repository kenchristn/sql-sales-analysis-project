# 📊 Sales & Support Data Analysis

SQL-based Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project to uncover business insights from sales transactions and support ticket data.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze:
- Sales performance
- Customer value
- Product performance
- Discount effectiveness
- Support operations

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- SQL (MySQL)
- Data Analysis
- Canva (for visualization)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 1. Revenue Concentration
- Revenue is highly dependent on a single customer (~68%)
- Indicates high business risk

### 2. Product Performance
- Low-priced products drive sales volume
- High-priced products generate most revenue

### 3. Discount Impact
- Higher discounts do not guarantee higher sales
- Product value is more important than pricing strategy

### 4. Support Performance
- Resolution rate is relatively high
- However, average resolution time is slow (~8.8 days)

### 5. Employee Performance
- Workload is unevenly distributed
- Performance gap exists between employees

---

## 🚀 Recommendations

- Implement customer segmentation (VIP, mid-tier, low-value)
- Use product bundling and cross-selling strategies
- Focus on value-based pricing instead of discounts
- Improve support efficiency and reduce resolution time
- Balance workload across employees
